ABOUT US

A web application which integrates stakeholders such as consumers, laboratories and government authorities by providing them a common platform.

MISSION AND VISION

Reduce health risks: By testing the food which people consume, pesticide levels can be identified. When people become aware of this, they can restrict themselves from consuming it.

TARGET AUDIENCE

A common platform for users that allows everyone to easy access of testing, analysis report, and complaint raising.

GOALS

✓ Easy access to the test and report.

✓ Creating awareness about the prevailing health problems due to pesticides.

✓ Making the consumer aware about the complaint system .
